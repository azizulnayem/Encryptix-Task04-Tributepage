Roman Reigns

WWE Superstar and Champion known for his incredible strength and dominant presence in the ring.

About the Superstar

Roman Reigns, born Leati Joseph "Joe" Anoa'i on May 25, 1985, in Pensacola, Florida, USA, is a professional wrestler and former football player. He is a member of the renowned Anoa'i wrestling family, which includes The Rock, Yokozuna, and Rikishi.

Early Life and Football Career

Reigns played college football for Georgia Tech and had brief off-season stints with the Minnesota Vikings and Jacksonville Jaguars before pursuing a career in professional wrestling.

Professional Wrestling Career

Roman Reigns debuted in WWE's developmental territory Florida Championship Wrestling (FCW) in 2010. He made his main roster debut as part of The Shield alongside Seth Rollins and Dean Ambrose in 2012, becoming one of the most dominant factions in WWE history.

Title Reigns

Roman Reigns has held multiple championships in WWE, including:

  • 4-time WWE World Heavyweight Champion
  • 2-time WWE Universal Champion
  • 1-time WWE Intercontinental Champion
  • 1-time WWE United States Champion
  • 1-time WWE Tag Team Champion (with Seth Rollins)
He is currently on a record-setting run with the Universal Championship, holding the title for over 1316 days.

Notable Achievements

  • 2015 Royal Rumble Winner
  • Headlined multiple WrestleManias
  • Named Wrestler of the Year by Pro Wrestling Illustrated in 2014
  • Ranked No. 1 in the PWI 500 in 2016

Personal Life and Legacy

In 2018, Reigns announced he was taking a leave of absence to battle leukemia, which he had been diagnosed with previously. He made a triumphant return to WWE in 2019 after his leukemia went into remission. Roman Reigns has also ventured into acting, appearing in the film "Fast & Furious Presents: Hobbs & Shaw" alongside his cousin, Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson.

Roman Reigns has been a dominant figure in WWE, known for his intense in-ring style and charismatic presence. He continues to be one of the most popular and influential wrestlers in the industry.
